GM Scope¶

VIP¶

GM will own the SW components which built the main and central VCU Statelogic and co-ordinate with SW parts on the SOC.

OS: AUTOSAR (Vector MICROSAR)¶

SystemStateController Determine and manage the system-state and propagate these to the state consumers in RTOS and Android.

SoC PLC State Controller Determine and manage the power-state of the VCU and propagate these to the state consumers in RTOS and Android.

SUM_SUSD SUM_SUSD is the StartupShutdown submodule of GM’s standard-utility-module library, coordinating the applicationst in terms of startup/shutdown with the underlying Autosar standard BSW modules and the StateControllers.
